Stitching confidence for women since 2012.

ESADI TAILOR - MIRIGAMA started on 16 October 2012 with a simple goal: to make women feel comfortable, confident and beautiful in clothes made specially for them.

Our Story

A ladies tailor shop built on trust.

Located on Pasyala Road, Mirigama, our shop focuses on ladies tailoring, custom stitching, bridal outfits and special outfit preparation. Every customer has different body shape, taste and comfort needs. That is why we listen carefully, take measurements properly and finish each outfit with attention.

Whether it is a bridal saree jacket, bridal frock, party dress, photo shoot dress or lehenga, our aim is to provide a friendly and respectful tailoring experience for women.
