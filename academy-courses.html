Academy / Courses

Tailoring course modules.

A structured practical learning path from basic machine handling to bridal saree jackets, party dresses and lehengas.

Basic Course

Foundation tailoring skills.

1

Machine Handling

Threading, straight stitching, control and safe machine use.

2

Measurements

Shoulder, bust, waist, hip, armhole, sleeve and length measuring.

3

Basic Cutting

Pattern basics and fabric cutting for simple ladies outfits.

Advanced Course

Bridal and special wear skills.

1

Bridal Saree Jacket

Pattern, fitting correction, lining, hooks and bridal finish.

2

Party Dresses

Frock designs, sleeves, necklines, flare and shape control.

3

Lehengas

Skirt cutting, top fitting and special occasion finishing.

Choose a course according to your level.

Beginners can start with foundation classes. Learners with experience can ask about bridal and advanced training.
